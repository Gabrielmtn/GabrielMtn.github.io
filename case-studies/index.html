Case studies · 2010–2026

Three projects from a sixteen-year career.

I'm a designer who codes, and an accessibility specialist who works at the engineering layer. I enjoy work that sits where those overlap: designing for how users actually think, validating a standard in code before I design against it, and building compliance into designs from the start rather than bolting it on after QA.

№ 01 View case study →

Virgin Pulse

Enterprise Wellness · 2021–2023 · UX Design Engineer

We don't control how content is consumed. Users do.
— Andrés Galante

Started with a Section 508 requirement for a Bank of America contract. Built a proof-of-concept using fluid layouts and relative units that became the standard operating procedure for 300+ engineers. Trained 400+ people across engineering, design, and product.

Putnam Investments

Financial Services · 2017 · Accessibility Engineer

Build the consistency keyboard users need, first.
— Working principle

Rebuilt dozens of interactive components (combobox, menu, dialog, disclosure) from WCAG keyboard interaction patterns and validated in JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver. Every component shipped with predictable keyboard interaction and correct state announcements.

Akamai Technologies

Infosec / Federal Cloud · 2019 · Sr. UI/UX Developer & Designer

Design for the mental model, not the domain.
— Working principle
